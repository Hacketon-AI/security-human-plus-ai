# Tenant-isolated administration and authenticated intelligence routes (v3)

This re-review confirms that a tenant administrator now derives all user-management authority from the organization in their validated token: registration ignores a supplied organization, and list, read, update, and delete operations scope their queries to that organization. AI Proof-of-Risk and domain-safe-scan now require `require_tenant_context`, while both frontend clients use the shared bearer-attaching transport. The credential-backed login/session restoration and the separate Overview, Operations, and AI Intelligence dashboard views remain wired as introduced. **Watch for:** **confirmed** the new authorization and session contracts still have no direct regression tests, so a later refactor could silently restore the closed cross-tenant or unauthenticated paths.
